Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide meaningful, updated information about the weather for Southeastern Idaho, as well as community news and events (festivals, elections, other activities) to unify and keep members informed.

Domain

I chose the domain: idahoweather.com. It is available for $7,750 plus $17.99/yr. This domain is a little more expensive, but I think it would be worth the initial pay to get this website started. It's an easy name to remember, and it's not too long.
