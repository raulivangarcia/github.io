Target Audience

Community members Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho between 12 and 70 years old. Also, tourists and visitors who may be attending community events or participating in recreational activities in Southeast Idaho.

Personas

Farmer Joe is a 57 year old who lives in Preston, Idaho. He owns his own farm and likes to be a part of the community. He needs to pay attention to the weather in order to take care of his crops, knowing when to plant and harvest, when to water more, and when to be careful of frost. He works hard, especially in the summer, but he makes time for community events and festivals.

Heidi Smith is a 45 year old woman who lives in Fish Haven, Idaho. She has a family business that rents and sells recreational equipment. Her shop is on the edge of Bear Lake, and she likes to check the weather to try and predict how well her business will do. Since Fish Haven is such a small town, she likes to attend community events to keep in touch with her neighbors.

Scenarios

What will the weather be like for my family vacation?
What special events are happening in the area?
What is the average rainfall in the area?
When does it usually start freezing in the area?
What is the weather going to be like for camping this weekend?
